Welcome to the Ziboinboin!

The Ziboinboin is a restaurant/shop located in the Limousin, in a town called Châteauneuf-la-Forêt. We've been going on since May 2009.

Our modus openrandi is to propose one set menu per day (with eventual variations for vegetarians and allergies). Each menu has for theme a country. You can also come and enjoy coffee, tea, milkshakes, and cakes in the afternoon. We also sell take-away food pots. Last but not least we make fun cocktails.

We also sell bags, aprons, and other hand sown items.

We open from May till November. For further information please check the opening hours and the menu.

We highly recommend making reservations in advance as we only have 16 seats. To make reservations please call us at 05.55.35.28.91.

The address of the restaurant is 18 Place de l'Église, 87130 Châteauneuf-la-Forêt.
